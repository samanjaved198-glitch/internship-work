About Us

About Bloom & Brew

Bloom & Brew is a cozy neighborhood café serving handcrafted coffee, fresh bakery items, and delicious meals made with love. Every cup is brewed to order and every plate is made fresh, right down the street from you.

We buy our beans directly from small farms, bake everything on-site before sunrise, and keep the room quiet enough for a real conversation.
